
Mary's father has 5 daughters but no sons : Nana,Nene, Nini, Nono. What is the fifth daughter's name probably ?

The fifth daughter's name is Nunu.

Why ?

There is a pattern to the names of the daughters. They all end in "n" sound. So, the fifth daughter's name is probably Nunu.------------------------------------

If you were running a race, and you passed the person in 2nd place, what place would you be in now ?

You would be in first place.

Why ?

If you passed the person in second place, it means you are now ahead of them. Therefore, you are in first place.------------------------------------
If you were running a race, and you passed the person in 2nd place, what place would you be in now ?

You would be in first place.

Why ?

If you passed the person in second place, that means you are now ahead of them. The first person is always in first place, the second person is always in second place, and so on. So if you are ahead of the second person, that means you are now in first place.------------------------------------
